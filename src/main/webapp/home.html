About

Welcome to our employee management system! This system is designed to help you manage all of your employees' information in one place, making it easier to keep track of important details such as their contact information, hire date, and more. With our system, you can easily add, edit, or delete employee records as needed, and view all of your employees at a glance.

How to Use the System

Using our system is easy! Simply navigate to the appropriate page using the links in the navigation bar above, and enter the requested information. You can add new employees, view all employees, delete employees, update employee information, and search for specific employees using the search bar.

System Requirements

Our system is designed to work with modern web browsers such as Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, and Microsoft Edge. You will need a stable internet connection to use the system.
